Artisan’s Tools::These Special tools include the items needed to pursue a craft or trade. The table shows examples of the most common types of tools, each providing items related to a single craft.
Disguise kit::This pouch of cosmetics, hair dye, and small props lets you create disguises that change your physical appearance.
Forgery kit::This small box contains a variety of papers and parchments, pens and inks, seals and Sealing wax, gold and silver leaf, and other supplies necessary to create convincing forgeries of physical documents.
Gaming Set::This item encompasses a wide range of game pieces, including dice and decks of cards (for games such as Three-Dragon Ante). A few common examples appear on the Tools table, but other kinds of gaming sets exist.
Herbalism kit::This kit contains a variety of instruments such as clippers, mortar and pestle, and pouches and vials used by herbalists to create remedies and potions.
Musical Instrument::Some of these instruments are Bagpipes, Drums, Dulcimer, Flute, Lute & Viol, etc...
Navigator’s Tools::This set of instruments is used for navigation at sea.
Poisoner’s Kit::A poisoner’s kit includes the vials, chemicals, and other Equipment necessary for the Creation of Poisons.
Thieves’ Tools::This set of tools includes a small file, a set of lock picks, a small mirror mounted on a metal handle, a set of narrow-bladed scissors, and a pair of pliers.
